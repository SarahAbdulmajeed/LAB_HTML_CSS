Types of Bouquets

Bouquets are a beautiful way to express emotions and mark special moments. They vary in structure and purpose. Here are the most common types:

  1. Handheld bouquet A bouquet held in the hand, often used by brides or as a personal gift. It allows for freedom in shape and individual touches.
  2. Base bouquet Flowers arranged on a firm base (foam or basket), commonly used as centerpieces for events or table decorations.
  3. Base bouquet with add-ons A base bouquet enhanced with symbolic elements like ribbons, small gifts, or money โ ideal for special celebrations such as graduations or welcoming a newborn.
  4. Hand-Tied Spiral Bouquet A bouquet constructed using the spiral-stem technique and tied by hand, creating a naturally flowing design.
